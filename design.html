Our Process

1

Discovery

We begin by understanding your business goals, target audience, and specific requirements for the theme.

2

Research

Analyzing competitor themes, industry standards, and user expectations to inform our design approach.

3

Wireframing

Creating basic layout structures and navigational frameworks to plan the user experience flow.

4

Design

Developing visual designs with color schemes, typography, imagery, and UI elements aligned with your brand.

5

Development

Coding the design into a functional, responsive theme with proper optimization and clean code.

6

Testing

Rigorous testing on multiple devices and browsers to ensure compatibility and performance.

7

Deployment

Installing the theme on your platform with proper configuration and content integration.

8

Support

Providing ongoing support, maintenance, and updates to keep your theme running smoothly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms do you design themes for?
We design themes for WordPress, Shopify, Squarespace, Webflow, and custom platforms. Our expertise spans across major CMS and e-commerce platforms to deliver optimized themes for your specific needs.
How long does it take to create a custom theme?
The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A basic theme can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while more complex themes with custom functionality may take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you offer theme maintenance after completion?
Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your theme updated, secure, and functioning optimally. This includes regular updates, compatibility checks, and technical support.
Can you redesign an existing theme?
Absolutely! We can work with your existing theme to improve its design, functionality, and performance. Our team can analyze your current theme and recommend the best approach for enhancement.
Are your themes responsive and mobile-friendly?
Yes, all our themes are built with a mobile-first approach ensuring they look and function perfectly across all devices including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.
